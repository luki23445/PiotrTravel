About PiotrTravel

Founded in 2005 by Piotr Nowak, PiotrTravel has grown to become Poland's leading pilgrimage and spiritual travel agency. Our mission is to create meaningful journeys that combine spiritual growth with cultural exploration.

With over 20 years of experience and more than 50,000 satisfied pilgrims, we specialize in creating thoughtfully planned itineraries to the world's most sacred destinations. Our team includes experienced guides, spiritual directors, and travel professionals dedicated to making your pilgrimage a transformative experience.
